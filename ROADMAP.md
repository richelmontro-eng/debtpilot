# DebtPilot Roadmap

Status legend: ✅ Completed · 🚧 In progress · 🗓️ Planned

## Milestone 1 — Foundation

- ✅ Email/password authentication and account management
- ✅ Cross-device email confirmation with cookie-backed sessions
- ✅ User-owned financial profiles and row-level data isolation
- ✅ Bills, debts, goals, transactions, vehicles, and settings workflows
- ✅ Reviewed transaction posting and balance reconciliation
- ✅ Guided first-time onboarding
- ✅ Responsive application shell and account controls

## Milestone 2 — Financial Intelligence

- ✅ Pilot Intelligence Engine with deterministic prioritization
- ✅ Explainable recommendations, confidence, impact, and completion history
- ✅ Financial Command Center briefing and health pulse
- ✅ Grouped financial timeline for bills, paychecks, and goal events
- ✅ Deterministic financial insights
- ✅ Missing Information Center
- ✅ Educational empty states and non-technical error handling
- ✅ Accessibility and mobile review of core intelligence panels
- ✅ Product vision, milestone roadmap, CEO observation log, and changelog

## Milestone 3 — Decision Center

- 🚧 What-if and payoff comparison foundations
- ✅ Before You Buy deterministic purchase decision report
- 🗓️ Saved decisions and scenario comparison history
- 🗓️ Tradeoff views across debt, cash reserves, and goals
- 🗓️ Decision reminders and outcome review
- 🗓️ Improved trend baselines and variance explanations

## Milestone 4 — Personal CFO

- ✅ Shared Pilot Intelligence Platform foundation
- ✅ Unified financial events and deterministic insight models
- ✅ Internal weekly brief generation service
- 🗓️ Rolling 30/60/90-day financial plan
- 🗓️ Proactive risk detection and prioritized planning agenda
- 🗓️ Life-event and irregular-income planning
- 🗓️ Household-level goals and shared planning
- 🗓️ Personalized cadence for weekly and monthly reviews

## Milestone 5 — Automation

- 🗓️ Permissioned account synchronization
- 🗓️ Automatic transaction classification with user review
- 🗓️ Balance, bill, and goal monitoring
- 🗓️ Safe reminders and approval-based action workflows
- 🗓️ Audit trail for automated and user-approved changes

## Milestone 6 — Premium Intelligence

- 🗓️ Advanced forecasting and uncertainty ranges
- 🗓️ Multi-scenario optimization
- 🗓️ Household collaboration and advisor-ready reports
- 🗓️ Premium alerts, benchmarks, and planning depth
